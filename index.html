Phase 01

Pre-Campaign

Everything we need in place before your campaign goes live. Hitting these deadlines ensures a smooth, on-time launch.

Signed Insertion Order (IO)
Must be received before campaign setup can begin — no campaign is trafficked without a countersigned IO.
3–5 days before go-live
Creative Assets & Tags
All final creatives, tags, and banner/video files. Checked immediately upon receipt for brand safety and technical compliance.
3–5 days before go-live
AI Insight Study Questions (if applicable)
Where an AI Insight Study is included in scope. 3 days is the absolute minimum — earlier is always preferred.
3–5 days before go-live
BLS Study Questions (if applicable)
Where a Brand Lift Study is included in scope. Questions must be confirmed ahead of launch to allow for integration and setup.
3–5 days before go-live
Bespoke Creative Builds
Where Alkimi is building or adapting creative formats. Rush builds available but require direct escalation.
5–10 days before go-live
Rush: 3–5 days
New Campaign Setup — Standard
Full trafficking, targeting configuration, and QA for a standard single-format campaign.
2–3 business days
New Campaign Setup — Complex / Multi-Format
Campaigns spanning multiple formats, geos, or audience segments. Rush setup requires escalation — please raise directly with your Alkimi CSM team.
3–5 business days
Rush: contact Alkimi CSM team
Kick-off Call
A campaign launch thread is set up for every campaign. A dedicated client kick-off call is available on request.
Pre-campaign
💡

Missing any of the above deadlines may push back your go-live date. Your CSA will flag any risk to launch timing as soon as it's identified and advise on revised timelines.
